PC & Mac Repairs

Our speciality is hardware and software repair. Most repairs are finished  and ready for pickup within 48 hours after you bring the computer into our shop. At that time you will be notified via call, text or email depending on what customer preference. Although prices are negotiable, thsee are our standard rates.

Data Recovery & Backup

We offer a data backup service in the event of your computer’s operating system reinstallation. For all types of files the charge for a temporary backup (just until it is put back on the same drive) is $1/GB copied. Another option is a permanent, encrypted backup based on a secure, non-networked PC. Anytime after you are in need of your files we can help. Pricing is $5/GB for encrypted data archiving. It is also sometimes possible to retrieve lost data on malfunctioned or wiped hard drives. We will attempt to retrieve non- corrupted files at no charge unless we are able to help. Data retrieval pricing is $20/GB restored.
